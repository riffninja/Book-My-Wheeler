FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your ride in Goa.

Book Your Ride
What documents are required to rent a vehicle?
You'll need a valid driving licence (DL), a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar card or passport), and one security deposit cheque. The cheque is held as a security measure and will not be cashed unless there is damage to the vehicle.
Is fuel included in the rental price?
No, fuel is not included. Vehicles are delivered with a full tank and should be returned with a full tank as well. This ensures transparency and fairness for all customers.
What is the minimum rental period?
The minimum rental period is 1 day (24 hours). We also offer attractive weekly and monthly packages — ask our team for special discounts on long-term rentals.
Do you deliver vehicles to hotels?
Absolutely! We deliver to your hotel, resort, villa, or any address in Goa. Simply mention your accommodation in the booking form and we'll handle the rest.
What if the vehicle breaks down?
We provide 24/7 roadside assistance. In the unlikely event of a breakdown, call our support line and we'll send help immediately. If needed, we'll provide a replacement vehicle so your trip isn't interrupted.
Can I extend my rental period?
Yes! Extensions are welcome. Simply call us before your scheduled return time and we'll arrange an extension subject to availability. Extended rentals may qualify for better daily rates.
